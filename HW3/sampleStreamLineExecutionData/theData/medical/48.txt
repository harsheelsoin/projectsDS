United States Department of Labor Wage and Hour Division (WHD) Family and Medical Leave Act Overview The FMLA entitles eligible employees of covered employers to take unpaid, job-protected leave for specified family and medical reasons with continuation of group health insurance coverage under the same terms and conditions as if the employee had not taken leave.
